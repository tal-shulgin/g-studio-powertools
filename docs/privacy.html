Privacy Policy for G-Studio PowerTools

Last updated: February 3, 2026

TL;DR: We collect absolutely nothing. Your data stays on your computer.

1. Information We Collect

None. G-Studio PowerTools operates entirely locally within your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Chat messages or conversation content
  • Prompts you save to the library
  • Boundary flags you set
  • Browsing history
  • Personal identifiers
  • Usage analytics

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally using:

  • chrome.storage.local - for your prompt library and settings
  • chrome.storage.session - for temporary state (if needed)

This data never leaves your device and is automatically cleared when you uninstall the extension.

3. Permissions Justification

Permission Why We Need It
storage To save your prompt library and boundary flags locally
activeTab To detect when you're on AI Studio and inject the sidebar
host_permissions (aistudio.google.com) Strictly limited to Google AI Studio to add delete buttons and sidebar

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Tracking pixels
  • CDN-hosted JavaScript (all code is bundled)
  • External APIs
